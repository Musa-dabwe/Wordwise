/*
 * Context7 Research Findings:
 * 1. Material 3 Forced Dark Theme Parent:
 *    - According to Material Components for Android documentation (MDC-Android), separate light and dark themes
 *      can be defined by inheriting from Theme.Material3.Light and Theme.Material3.Dark respectively.
 *    - For a forced-dark app that does not use DayNight, the canonical parent is "Theme.Material3.Dark.NoActionBar".
 *
 * 2. android:forceDarkAllowed Attribute:
 *    - Per Android Developer documentation, android:forceDarkAllowed (API 29+) should be set to "false"
 *      for apps that already provide their own dark theme. This prevents the system's "Force Dark" feature
 *      from automatically modifying the app's colors, which could lead to visual bugs or inconsistent branding.
 */
